# 🌿 Home Micropropagation

Welcome to my open-source plant tissue culture research repository. I am documenting my self-directed study in botanical micropropagation, tracking culture media variations, and optimizing tissue culture protocols right from my bedroom.

## 📊 Current Focus
- **Target Species: Tradescantia Pallida**
- **Current Objective:** Establishing a proper protocol for Tradescantia.
- **Tools Used:** Obsidian, GitHub, DIY Still Air Box.

## 📁 Repository Map
- `Experiments/`: Active and completed tissue culture trials.
- `Media Batches/`: Detailed chemical breakdown of my growth media recipes ($\text{mg/L}$ hormone ratios).
- `Photo Inventory/`: Chronological growth timelines.

## 📈 Open Statement
Every lab entry in this repository is mirrored directly from my physical laboratory notebook. This is done to appreciate the art of micropropagation and to demonstrate that tissue culture is a hobby anyone can enjoy, even on a student's budget. Because the tools I use are likely to be non-professional or DIY, please feel free to reach out or chat with me if there is anything worth sharing. I'd love to discuss tissue culture as a peer or a student.
